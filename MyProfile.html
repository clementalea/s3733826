Favorite Sport

My favorite sport is basketball. No matter how good a single player is, he still has to operate as only a single part of a large network, and the best plays in the history of the sport, in my opinion, has to do with a dynamic beauty that emerges from only the best chemistry and teamwork that a squad of 5-men running on hardwood can produce.
